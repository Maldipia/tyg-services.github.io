Simple Systems

You will learn to track:

  • Sales — what sold, when, to whom
  • Payments — who paid, who hasn't
  • Mistakes — what went wrong, why
  • Progress — are you improving?

No advanced tools required. Notebook or Google Sheets is enough.

About Mistakes

Mistakes are not failures.

Untracked mistakes become failures.

We learn by:

  • Noticing errors
  • Understanding why
  • Correcting the process
  • Improving the system

When Systems Become Important

Systems matter most when:

  • Sales become consistent
  • Time becomes limited
  • Errors start repeating
  • You can't keep track in your head

This is when structure saves you.
