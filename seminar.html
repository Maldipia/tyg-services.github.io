Is This For You?

We value quality over quantity. This workshop is designed for a specific type of person.

This IS For You If:

  • You are a beginner with low capital but high willingness to learn.
  • You are an OFW looking for a manageable side business.
  • You want to learn actual business skills (sales, tracking, operations).
  • You value community support and honest feedback.

This is NOT For You If:

  • You are looking for "easy money" or "passive income" without work.
  • You are not willing to track your sales and expenses daily.
  • You expect automation to solve problems you haven't understood yet.
  • You are looking for a "get rich quick" scheme.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a guaranteed income program?

No. Business results depend entirely on your effort, market conditions, and execution. We provide the training and access to products; you provide the work.

Do I need to buy software or automation tools?

No. We teach you to use free tools like Google Sheets first. Automation is an optional advanced step for when you are ready to scale.

Is there a fee to join the reseller program?

The seminar is free. Acceptance into the reseller program is based on your commitment and performance during the workshop.

I'm an OFW, can I do this?

Yes! This program is specifically designed to be manageable remotely, provided you have internet access and the discipline to communicate.
